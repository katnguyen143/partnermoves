Playful Movement for All Levels

Playful, Social Movement

Partner Moves is all about connection through movement. We make wellness fun and social with practices like Acroyoga, Thai massage, flying therapeutics (lunar Acroyoga), and partner stretches.

Whether you're joining us at a jam, online, or on retreat, you'll find clear guidance, lots of laughter, and a community that cheers for you.

Learn With Tutorials

Our tutorials break down movements in a simple, welcoming way so you can try new skills at your own pace. Every video is beginner-friendly, clearly explained, and focused on safety and communication.

Whether you're exploring your first counterbalance or refining transitions, you'll always find guidance that feels doable and fun.
